## Introduction
This document catalogs authentic archaeological artifacts representing Viking Age kingship, royal authority, and political power structures. All artifacts are verified museum objects with documented provenance.

## Historical Context
The Viking Age (793-1066 CE) saw the development of complex political structures in Scandinavia, from local chieftains to powerful kings ruling unified territories. These artifacts demonstrate the material culture of royal power and legitimacy.
