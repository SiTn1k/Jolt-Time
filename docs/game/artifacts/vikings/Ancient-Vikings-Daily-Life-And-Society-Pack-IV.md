# Ancient Vikings — Daily Life & Society (Pack IV)

## Introduction
This document catalogs authentic archaeological artifacts representing the everyday life, social structures, and material culture of Viking Age Scandinavia. All objects are verified through museum records and academic publications.

## Historical Context
From 793-1066 CE, Viking society encompassed farmers, craftsmen, traders, and explorers. These artifacts reveal their domestic life, subsistence strategies, and social organization beyond the warrior stereotype.
