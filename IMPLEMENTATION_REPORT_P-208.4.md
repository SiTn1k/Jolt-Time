## Quality Assurance

### Historical Verification
- All artifacts are documented in museum collections or peer-reviewed archaeological publications
- Primary sources include British Museum, Louvre, University of Pennsylvania, Yale Babylonian Collection

Each artifact includes:
- Official Name

- Historical Period
- Approximate Date
- Current Museum

- Daily Life Context

- Interesting Facts
- Educational Summary
- References
