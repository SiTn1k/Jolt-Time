# Jolt Time — Revenue Intelligence Dashboard Architecture

## Overview

**Purpose:** Define the complete architectural framework for the Revenue Intelligence Dashboard, establishing it as the central business intelligence and decision-making system for the entire project.

**Scope:** This document establishes the Revenue Intelligence Dashboard as the central business command center, covering dashboard categories, philosophy, architecture layers, dashboard systems, intelligence standards, analytics, governance, and future expansion paths.

**Principle:** This dashboard should become the central business intelligence and decision-making system for the entire project. The dashboard must provide a unified view of revenue, retention, engagement, growth, monetization, and operational performance. The architecture should support future scaling from thousands to millions of users.
