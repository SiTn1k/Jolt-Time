# Jolt Time — Creator System and Community Contributions

## Overview

The Jolt Time creator system recognizes and celebrates community members who contribute creativity, knowledge, and positive energy to the project. Creator systems are long-term features designed to strengthen community bonds and reward players who help expand the world of Jolt Time.

**Core Philosophy:** Creators should help expand the world of Jolt Time through creativity, community participation, and educational contributions. All creator rewards remain cosmetic, and AdsGram remains the primary revenue system that sustains the project.

---

## 1. Creator Philosophy

The creator philosophy establishes guiding principles for community contributions.

### Core Principles

Jolt Time should encourage:

- **Creativity:** Expressing artistic and design talents within the Jolt Time universe
- **Community Participation:** Engaging with fellow players to build positive experiences
- **Educational Contributions:** Sharing historical knowledge and scholarly insights
- **World Expansion:** Helping to grow and enrich the Jolt Time universe

### Creator Role

**Creators help expand the world of Jolt Time:**

- Fan content that enriches the game universe
- Historical context that enhances educational value
- Community events that bring players together
- Artistic contributions that showcase talent
- Collaborative projects that build community bonds

### Non-Commercial Focus

**Creator system is not a commercial venture:**

- No direct revenue sharing (future revenue sharing documented separately)
- Recognition and appreciation primary rewards
- Community building rather than monetization focus
- Organic growth through genuine appreciation

### Inclusivity

- All players can potentially become creators
- No minimum skill threshold for participation
- Multiple paths to creator recognition
- Encouragement over gatekeeping

## 7. Creator Guidelines

Content guidelines ensure positive community contributions.

### Historical Respect

**Content should respect history:**

- Accurate representation of historical facts
- Sensitivity toward cultural significance
- Acknowledgment of historical context
- Avoidance of historical distortions
- Educational tone for historical content

### Positive Content

**Content should avoid harmful material:**

- No hate speech or discrimination
- No explicit or adult content
- No harassment or bullying
- No misinformation or conspiracy theories
- No harmful stereotypes

### Community Support

**Content should support positive communities:**

- Encouraging and uplifting tone
- Constructive feedback culture
- Inclusivity and diversity
- Respectful disagreement
- Collaborative spirit

### Quality Standards

**Content should meet quality bar:**

- Original work or properly attributed
- Clear and understandable
- Respectful of player community
- Aligned with Jolt Time values
- Appropriate for all ages

### Submission Process

- **Review:** All submissions reviewed for guidelines compliance
- **Feedback:** Constructive feedback for improvements
- **Appeal:** Process for challenging rejection
- **Improvement:** Support for quality improvement

## 10. AdsGram Integration

Creator systems must never replace AdsGram as the primary revenue system.

### Revenue System Hierarchy

1. **AdsGram (Primary):** Main revenue source for project sustainability
2. **Subscriptions (Secondary):** Jolt Time Plus premium membership
3. **Creator System (Tertiary):** Community recognition and appreciation
4. **Future Revenue Sharing (Future):** Potential creator compensation

### Creator System and AdsGram

**Creator systems do not impact AdsGram:**

- No ads replaced by creator content
- No AdsGram revenue directed to creators
- Creator recognition independent of ads
- Project sustainability first priority

### AdsGram Revenue Protection

**AdsGram remains core business model:**

- Creator system adds value without replacing ads
- Community building enhances retention, not revenue
- Creator appreciation funded through AdsGram success
- No commercial pressure on creator system

### Creator System Sustainability

- Self-sustaining through community participation
- No direct project funding required
- Rewards primarily recognition-based
- Organic growth encouraged
