# Jolt Time — Capsule System

## Overview

The Historical Capsule system is the primary method for obtaining artifacts. Capsules are earned through gameplay, daily rewards, quest completion, and AdsGram rewarded ads. The system is designed to be fair, transparent, and always rewarding—no gambling mechanics, no forced purchases.

**Core Principle**: Every capsule should feel like a gift. Players should always progress.

---

## Capsule Philosophy

### Fair Design Principles
- ✅ No forced purchases to progress
- ✅ Clear drop rates always visible
- ✅ Guaranteed progression every session
- ✅ AdsGram rewarded ads as primary acquisition method
- ✅ Daily free capsules always available
- ❌ No loot boxes with hidden odds
- ❌ No time-limited pressure to buy
- ❌ No gambling-style mechanics
- ❌ No pay-to-win advantages

### Player Experience Goals
- Open capsule → Feel rewarded
- Collect artifacts → See progress
- Complete sets → Celebrate achievement
- Watch ad → Get fair reward

```

   - Highlight effect activates
   
2. ACTIVATION (1.0s)
   - Capsule glows and shakes
   - Energy particles gather
   - Sound builds anticipation
   
3. RELEASE (1.5s)
   - Capsule bursts open
   - Artifact rises with light beam
   - Rarity-colored particles explode
   
4. REVEAL (1.0s)
   - Artifact fully revealed
   - Rarity banner appears

5. COLLECTION (0.5s)

## AdsGram Integration

### Primary Revenue Model
**AdsGram is the main monetization strategy.**

### Ad Types for Capsules

#### Rewarded Video Ads
```
Trigger: Player-initiated
Reward: Basic or Rare Capsule
Frequency: Unlimited (4-hour cooldown)
Guarantee: At least Common
```

#### Interstitial Ads
```
Trigger: Natural game breaks
Reward: None (revenue only)
Frequency: Max 1 per 3 minutes
Location: Between missions
```

```
