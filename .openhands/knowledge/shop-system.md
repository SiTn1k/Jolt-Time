# Jolt Time — Shop System

## Overview

The Jolt Time Shop provides players with optional cosmetic purchases and convenience items that enhance their experience without creating pay-to-win advantages. **AdsGram remains the primary revenue source.** All shop purchases are purely cosmetic or convenience-focused. Every gameplay-affecting reward remains earnable through free play.

**Core Philosophy:**
- Players are NEVER required to spend money
- All cosmetics are purely visual — no gameplay advantages
- Free players can acquire everything through gameplay
- Shop enhances experience without dominating it
