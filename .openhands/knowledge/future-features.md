## Feature Prioritization Criteria

### Impact Factors
1. **Player Value** — How much does this improve player experience?
2. **Development Cost** — Time and resources required
3. **Technical Risk** — Complexity and uncertainty
4. **Revenue Potential** — Impact on monetization
5. **Strategic Alignment** — Fit with long-term vision

### Priority Matrix

| Impact / Cost | Low Cost | High Cost |
|--------------|----------|-----------|
| **High Impact** | Do First | Do Next |
| **Low Impact** | Fill-in | Deprioritize |
