# Battle Pass System

## Overview

Jolt Time's Battle Pass is a seasonal progression system that rewards dedication and engagement throughout each season. The Battle Pass provides structured goals, cosmetic rewards, and a sense of achievement without creating pay-to-win advantages. Every player receives meaningful rewards through the free track, while the premium track offers enhanced cosmetics for those who choose to upgrade.

**Key Principles:**
- All gameplay-affecting rewards are free to all players
- Premium track contains cosmetic-only enhancements
- Seasons align with real-world quarters (approximately 90 days / 3 months)
- Progression is achievable for casual players with regular engagement
- No mandatory purchases ever required

## 12. Battle Pass Philosophy

Jolt Time's Battle Pass embodies principles of fair progression, meaningful rewards, and respectful engagement.

### Core Principles

#### Reward Active Players
- Daily engagement rewarded with steady progression
- Weekend bonuses encourage play during free time
- Seasonal themes create anticipation and collection goals
- Clear progression path with visible milestones

#### Respect Player Time
- Core progression achievable in 15-30 minutes daily
- Catch-up mechanics prevent FOMO anxiety
- No mandatory daily login required
- Weekend warriors can still progress
- Missed days don't ruin seasons

#### Avoid Pay-to-Win
- All gameplay-affecting rewards are free
- Premium track is cosmetic-only
- No energy shortcuts through purchase
- No rating advantages through purchase
- Equal competitive footing for all

#### Support Casual Players
- Level 40 achievable for casual players
- Level 50 requires regular engagement
- Bonus levels reward dedicated players
- Catch-up mechanics help late joiners
- Season archive preserves legacy

### Player Journey

| Player Type | Daily Time | Season Outcome |
|-------------|------------|----------------|
| Casual | 10-15 min | Level 35-45 |
| Regular | 20-30 min | Level 45-55 |
| Dedicated | 45-60 min | Level 55-70 |
| Hardcore | 90+ min | Level 70+ |

### Anti-Frustration Design

| Concern | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Missed Days | Catch-up mechanics, weekend boosts |
| Behind Pace | Alert system, catch-up calculator |
| Can't Finish | Level 40 guaranteed with 50% dailies |
| Missed Season | New season starts fresh, archive preserved |
| Premium Regret | 24-hour refund window |

### XP Calculation

```sql

SELECT 
  level,
  current_xp,
  xp_required_for_next_level(level) as xp_needed,
  (current_xp * multiplier) as effective_xp
FROM player_battle_pass
WHERE player_id = $1 AND season = $2;
```
