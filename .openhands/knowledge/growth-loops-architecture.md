# Jolt Time — Growth Loops Architecture

## Overview

**Purpose:** Define the complete architectural framework for Growth Loops, establishing organic growth as a primary engine that continuously generates new users, increases engagement, improves retention, and strengthens the ecosystem.

**Scope:** This document establishes Growth Loops as a major growth engine, covering loop categories, philosophy, architecture layers, loop mechanisms, integration standards, analytics, governance, and future expansion paths.

**Principle:** Growth Loops should become one of the primary engines of organic growth. The architecture should create self-sustaining cycles that continuously generate new users, increase engagement, improve retention, and strengthen the ecosystem. Growth loops must be scalable, measurable, and sustainable. The framework should support future growth from thousands to millions of users.
