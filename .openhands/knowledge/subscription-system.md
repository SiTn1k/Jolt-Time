# Jolt Time — Subscription System and Jolt Time Plus

## Overview

Jolt Time Plus is the premium subscription service that rewards loyal players with convenience features and cosmetic benefits. Subscriptions improve player comfort without creating gameplay advantages — all core progression remains fully accessible to free players.

**Core Philosophy:** Subscriptions should improve comfort and support the project. Players without subscriptions enjoy the complete game experience. AdsGram remains the primary revenue system, with Jolt Time Plus as a supplementary premium option.

---

## 1. Subscription Philosophy

The subscription philosophy establishes guiding principles for Jolt Time Plus.

### Core Principles

Jolt Time Plus should:

- **Provide Convenience:** Make gameplay more comfortable, not more powerful
- **Reward Loyal Players:** Recognize and appreciate ongoing support
- **Remain Completely Optional:** No gameplay content requires subscription
- **Never Create Pay-to-Win:** No combat advantages or progression shortcuts

### Free Player Parity

**Players without subscriptions enjoy full game access:**

- All artifacts available through gameplay
- All eras unlockable through progression
- All competitive modes accessible
- All collection achievements achievable
- No gameplay content locked behind subscription

### Subscription Boundaries

**Jolt Time Plus enhances but never gates:**

- Cosmetic rewards and profile customization
- Convenience features like extra inventory slots
- Daily bonus resources (cosmetic or soft currency)
- Priority access to new features (never exclusive access)

### Value Proposition

- Clear, understandable benefits
- Fair pricing for the value provided
- Easy cancellation without penalty
- Immediate access upon subscription
- Transparent renewal process

## 9. Cancellation Philosophy

Player-friendly cancellation ensures trust and respect.

### Cancellation Freedom

**Players should be able to cancel anytime:**

- **Immediate Cancellation:** Cancel auto-renew anytime
- **Full Access Until End:** Continue benefits through billing period
- **No Penalty:** No cancellation fees or penalties
- **Easy Reactivation:** Resume subscription anytime

### Core Progression Preservation

**Cancelling never punishes players:**

- **Artifacts Retained:** All earned artifacts remain
- **Collection Progress:** All collection progress preserved
- **Competitive Standing:** No removal from leaderboards
- **Cosmetic Ownership:** Keep all earned cosmetics

### Earned Rewards Keep

**Players retain what they've earned:**

- **Cosmetic Items:** All subscriber cosmetics remain usable
- **Loyalty Badges:** All milestone badges retained
- **Bonus Resources:** All claimed bonus resources kept
- **Inventory Slots:** Extra inventory slots become unavailable but items remain

### Post-Cancellation Experience

- **Standard Player:** Treated as free player
- **Rejoin Option:** Easy subscription reactivation
- **Welcome Back:** No penalty for previous cancellation
- **Loyalty Recognition:** Past subscription time counted toward future milestones

## 10. Telegram Bot Notifications

Bot notifications keep subscribers informed about their subscription status.

### Subscription Renewal

**Trigger:** Subscription is about to renew

- Current plan and renewal date
- Renewal amount
- Benefits continuing
- Quick link to manage subscription

**Frequency:** 7 days before, 3 days before, and day of renewal

### Expiration Reminders

**Trigger:** Subscription approaching expiration

- Days remaining
- Benefits expiring
- Option to renew at discount (if applicable)
- Option to cancel auto-renew

**Frequency:** 14 days, 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before expiration

### Premium Rewards

**Trigger:** New daily or monthly premium reward available

- Reward description
- Claim button/link
- Reward value
- Time remaining to claim

**Frequency:** As rewards become available, once per reward

### Subscription Status

**Trigger:** Important subscription status change

- Subscription activated
- Payment failed (with retry option)
- Subscription cancelled confirmation
- Benefits restored after payment issue

**Frequency:** Immediate for status changes

### Notification Guidelines

**Never become spam. Strict controls apply:**

- Maximum 4 subscription notifications per week
- Fully customizable notification preferences
- Quiet hours respected
- Critical notifications (payment issues) always delivered
- One-click unsubscribe from non-essential notifications

## 11. AdsGram Integration

Jolt Time Plus must not remove AdsGram — AdsGram remains the primary revenue system.

### Revenue System Hierarchy

1. **AdsGram (Primary):** Main revenue source for project sustainability
2. **Jolt Time Plus:** Premium subscription for convenience
3. **Telegram Stars Purchases:** Optional one-time purchases
4. **Future TON Integration:** Web3 payment services

### AdsGram and Jolt Time Plus Relationship

**Jolt Time Plus must NOT remove AdsGram completely:**

- Subscribers still see ads, but potentially at reduced frequency
- Ads remain the primary revenue model
- No subscriber tier completely removes advertisements
- Project sustainability depends on AdsGram revenue

### Subscriber Ad Experience

**Reduced but respectful ad experience:**

- **Reduced Frequency:** Subscribers may see fewer ads (not zero)
- **Optional Viewing:** Subscribers can choose to watch ads for bonus rewards
- **Respectful Format:** No intrusive or excessive ad formats
- **Always Optional:** Watching ads never required for any benefit

### AdsGram Revenue Protection

**AdsGram remains core business model:**

- Development primarily funded by AdsGram
- Jolt Time Plus supplements but doesn't replace AdsGram
- No aggressive monetization to compensate for reduced ads
- Fair player experience maintained for all

### Bonus Optional Rewards

**Subscribers may receive:**

- **Reduced Ad Frequency:** Fewer ads during gameplay
- **Bonus Optional Rewards:** Extra rewards when choosing to watch ads
- **Improved Convenience:** Skip options more available
- **Ad-Free Breaks:** Short ad-free sessions as random bonuses

## 12. Fair Monetization Philosophy

Jolt Time maintains ethical monetization practices that respect all players.

### Fairness Principles

Jolt Time should:

- **Avoid Pay-to-Win:** No gameplay power purchasable
- **Respect Free Players:** Free experience is complete and rewarding
- **Reward Supporters:** Clear value for subscription price
- **Maintain Balance:** No competitive advantages from spending

### Free Player Experience

**Free players receive full game:**

- All core gameplay accessible
- All progression achievable
- Competitive modes fair and balanced
- Community fully inclusive

### Subscriber Experience

**Subscribers receive premium treatment:**

- Convenience and comfort improvements
- Cosmetic expression options
- Recognition and appreciation
- Priority support and early access

### Value Comparison

| Player Type | Gameplay Access | Progression | Competitive | Cosmetics |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Free | Full | Full | Fair | Basic |
| Subscriber | Full + Convenience | Full + Comfort | Fair + Support | Premium |

### Anti-Pay-to-Win Enforcement

- **No Power Purchases:** Combat strength cannot be bought
- **No Progress Shortcuts:** All content reachable without spending
- **Skill-Based Competition:** Competitive modes reward skill only
- **Cosmetic Differentiation:** Visual differences only, no power gaps
