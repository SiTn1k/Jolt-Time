This document serves as the single source of truth for all narrative decisions in Jolt Time. It establishes the foundational lore, universe rules, and narrative standards that govern storytelling across all game content, events, and future expansions.

**Core Mission:** Jolt Time celebrates humanity's shared history while inspiring curiosity and preserving educational value. Every narrative decision must align with these principles.

## Part II: Lore Categories

### 1. World Overview

#### The Year 2157

The setting is a future where humanity has achieved advanced technological capabilities, including consciousness-based time travel. The world has developed sophisticated quantum computing, sustainable energy systems, and global cooperation frameworks. Despite technological advancement, social and cultural divisions persist.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Global communication networks spanning Earth and orbital habitats
- Medical advancements enabling 150-year average lifespans
- Historical preservation as a primary cultural value
- Established temporal research institutions

#### The Jolt Engine

The central technology of Jolt Time is the **Jolt Engine**—a consciousness-based time travel device developed by Chronos Corporation. Unlike physical time travel, the Jolt Engine projects user consciousness into historical moments while maintaining physical presence in the present-day Time Hub.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Consciousness projection range: 10,000 BCE to 2500 CE (calibrated eras)
- Temporal stability: 99.97% without protective suits
- Energy source: Temporal particles harvested from era boundaries
- Safety protocols: Mandatory temporal anchoring every 72 hours

**Operational Philosophy:**
- The Jolt Engine does not allow physical object extraction from past eras
- Consciousness projections can interact with historical environments
- Temporal paradoxes create "echoes" rather than timeline destruction
- Each user requires temporal compatibility testing before deployment

#### The Time Hub

The Time Hub serves as the primary base of operations for all Temporal Agents. It exists in a stabilized pocket dimension at the intersection of multiple timeline branches.

**Physical Characteristics:**
- Floating circular station with panoramic temporal views
- Central command center with real-time timeline monitoring
- Equipment fabrication and upgrade facilities
- Agent living quarters and recreation areas
- Museum wing displaying collected artifacts (as temporal echoes)

**Functional Areas:**

| Area | Purpose | Access |
|------|---------|--------|
| Command Center | Mission briefing, temporal monitoring | All agents |
| Jolt Chamber | Time travel departure/arrival | Authorized agents |
| Fabricator Bay | Equipment creation and upgrades | Certified technicians |
| Museum Wing | Artifact echo displays, education | All agents |
| Agent Quarters | Rest, preparation, socialization | Assigned agents |
| Research Wing | New era development, analysis | Research personnel |

### 2. Timeline

#### Temporal Structure

The Jolt Time universe operates on a **Branching Timeline Model** with a primary timeline and stable branches. The Temporal Shockwave created temporary instabilities, but the timeline has since stabilized.

**Timeline Hierarchy:**

| Level | Description | Stability |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Primary Timeline | The "correct" historical record | Maximum stability |
| Stable Branches | Authorized alternate history scenarios | High stability |
| Temporal Echoes | Ghost images of altered events | Unstable, non-interactive |
| Anomaly Zones | Shockwave remnants | Requires resolution |

**Temporal Rules:**
1. Agents cannot fundamentally alter historical outcomes
2. Minor interactions create "echoes" that fade over time
3. Artifact fragments are the only physical matter that crosses time
4. Paradoxes manifest as temporal storms, not timeline destruction
