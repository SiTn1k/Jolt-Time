# Jolt Time — Economy and Currency System

## Overview

The Jolt Time economy is designed around three core principles: **fairness**, **sustainability**, and **player respect**. The game uses a multi-currency system where every currency has a distinct purpose, clear earning paths, and meaningful spending options. **AdsGram is the primary revenue source** for the project — players earn in-game rewards through ads, but the monetary value flows to sustaining development, not to players.

This document defines the complete economy and currency systems for Jolt Time.

---

## 1. Currency Architecture

### 1.1 Currency Types

| Type | Description | Earned by Playing | Purchasable |
|------|-------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Main Currencies** | Core gameplay resources | ✅ | ❌ |
| **Premium Currencies** | Exclusive items and cosmetics | Limited | ✅ |
| **Soft Currencies** | Progression and collection tracking | ✅ | ❌ |
| **Event Currencies** | Limited-time content | ✅ | ❌ |

### 1.2 Currency Design Principles

1. **No Pay-to-Win** — No currency can purchase gameplay power or competitive advantages
2. **Clear Purpose** — Each currency has a unique, non-overlapping function
3. **Earned Through Play** — All currencies are accessible to free players
4. **Sustainable Sinks** — Every currency has spending outlets that prevent hoarding
5. **Transparent Values** — Players always know what they can get and what it costs
6. **Anti-Inflation Controls** — Caps, limits, and sinks keep the economy stable long-term

## 7. AdsGram Monetization

### 7.1 Revenue Model Overview

**AdsGram is the PRIMARY revenue source for Jolt Time.**

```
PROJECT INCOME FLOW:
AdsGram SDK → Players watch ads → Revenue generated → Project receives payment
                ↓
        In-game rewards granted
        (Players do NOT receive money)
```

### 7.2 Key Principles

> **PROJECT earns from AdsGram. PLAYERS do NOT earn money from AdsGram.**

- Players receive in-game currency and bonuses for watching ads
- Revenue (real money) flows entirely to project development
- Players cannot convert ad-watching into real currency
- AdsGram SDK handles ad serving, tracking, and payment

### 7.3 Ad Types and Player Rewards

| Ad Type | Trigger | Player Reward | Project Revenue |
|---------|---------|---------------|----------------|
| Rewarded Video | Player-initiated | Time Shards, Chrono Coins, Energy | High CPM ($8-12) |
| Interstitial | Natural breaks | None (project revenue only) | Medium CPM ($3-6) |
| Daily Bonus | Player-initiated | Enhanced daily reward | High engagement |
| Event Ad | Player-initiated | Event Tokens, bonus rewards | Premium event rates |

### 7.4 Player Rewards from Ads (In-Game Only)

| Reward Type | Amount | Currency |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| Extra Time Shards | 2-10 | Time Shards |
| Bonus Chrono Coins | 25-150 | Chrono Coins |
| Additional Energy | 20-50 | Energy |
| Event Token Boost | 25-100 | Event Tokens |
| Pack Upgrade | 1 tier | — |
| Double Reward | 2x | — |

### 7.5 AdsGram Rules

1. **Player Choice** — Rewarded ads are always player-initiated
2. **No Forced Viewing** — Ads are never required for progression
3. **Clear Value** — Rewards shown before ad viewing
4. **Skip Option** — Interstitial ads skippable after 5 seconds
5. **Frequency Cap** — Maximum 5 rewarded ads/day, 8 interstitials/hour
6. **Natural Breaks Only** — No ads during active gameplay
7. **No Real Money** — Players cannot earn or withdraw real currency

### 7.6 Compliance

- Official AdsGram SDK only
- Accurate impression reporting
- No ad fraud or manipulation
- Telegram ad policy compliance
- GDPR-ready data handling
- User privacy protection

## 10. Progression Philosophy

### 10.1 Fair Progression

**Players must be able to achieve everything through gameplay.**

| Commitment Level | Weekly Playtime | Monthly Progression |
|------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Casual (F2P) | 2-4 hours | 1-3 artifact upgrades, 5-10% collection |
| Regular (F2P) | 5-10 hours | 3-5 artifact upgrades, 10-20% collection |
| Dedicated (F2P) | 10-20 hours | 5-10 upgrades, 20-35% collection |
| Subscribers | Any | Cosmetic variety, minor convenience |

### 10.2 Grind-Friendly Design

**Time investment should always feel rewarding.**

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Consistent Returns | Every session yields visible progress |
| Milestone Markers | Short-term, medium-term, long-term goals |
| No Dead Ends | Alternative paths when one is blocked |
| Visible Progress | Clear indicators of advancement |
| Meaningful Choices | Currency decisions matter |

### 10.3 Non Pay-to-Win Commitment

**Money enhances experience, never gameplay power.**

| Allowed | Never Allowed |
|---------|---------------|
| Cosmetic purchases | Buying Time Energy |
| Convenience features | Buying artifacts |
| Cosmetic bundles | Buying competitive advantages |
| Supporting the project | Buying mission completions |
| Expressing support | Buying era unlocks |

### 10.4 Long-Term Sustainability

**The game must remain engaging for years.**

| Horizon | Player Goal | Game Support |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Week 1 | Learn mechanics | Tutorials, easy quests |
| Month 1 | Collect first artifacts | Daily engagement, starter packs |
| Month 6 | Complete first era | Mid-game content, events |
| Year 1 | Multiple eras | Advanced content, PvP |
| Year 2+ | Collection mastery | Prestige, competitive, events |

### 10.5 Balance Manifesto

```
EVERYTHING EARNABLE = EVERYTHING BUYABLE
         ↓
   COSMETICS ONLY
         ↓
   NO POWER = NO P2W
         ↓
   FAIR FOR ALL
         ↓
   SUSTAINABLE ECONOMY
```
