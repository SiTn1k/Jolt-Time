# Jolt Time — Project Vision and Core Principles

## Overview

This document describes what Jolt Time stands for and why the project exists. It serves as a reference for all future decisions, ensuring the project remains true to its identity and values.

**Core Philosophy:** Jolt Time exists to make history exciting, accessible, and fun for everyone. We believe learning about the past should feel like an adventure, not a chore.

---

## Mission Statement

### Why Jolt Time Exists

Jolt Time exists to spark curiosity about history in a new generation of learners. We believe that understanding where we came from helps us understand where we're going.

**Our mission is threefold:**

1. **Educate through entertainment** — History comes alive through gameplay, making learning feel like play rather than study.

2. **Democratize knowledge** — Everyone deserves access to fascinating historical content, regardless of background or education.

3. **Build lasting memories** — We create experiences players will remember fondly for years, associating positive feelings with learning.

### What Value Jolt Time Provides

**For Players:**

- An engaging escape into worlds they might never have explored
- The satisfaction of building collections and completing sets
- Social connections through shared experiences
- A gentle introduction to historical facts without academic pressure

**For the Broader World:**

- Increased historical awareness and cultural literacy
- Preservation of historical knowledge through modern medium
- Inspiration to learn more about the past
- A model for educational entertainment done right

### What Makes Jolt Time Unique

**Time Travel as a Framework:**

- Eras serve as natural progression milestones
- Historical accuracy grounds the fantastical elements
- The concept appeals universally across cultures

**Collectible Depth:**

- Artifacts provide tangible goals and rewards
- Collecting creates emotional investment
- Historical context adds meaning to items

**Social Experience:**

- Shared experiences strengthen community bonds
- Cooperative goals encourage teamwork
- Healthy competition drives engagement

## Guiding Questions

When facing difficult decisions, these questions help ensure alignment with the vision:

**Does this serve the player?**
Will this make the player experience better, or just make us more money?

**Does this respect the player's time?**
Would a player thank us for this feature, or resent the obligation?

**Does this maintain trust?**
Would we be proud to tell players exactly how this works?

**Does this preserve quality?**
Would this meet our standards if it shipped today?

**Does this fit the vision?**
Would this make Jolt Time more or less aligned with why we exist?
