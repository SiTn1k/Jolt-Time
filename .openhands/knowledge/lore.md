# Jolt Time — Lore & Story

## The Discovery

In the year 2157, humanity had conquered time itself—or so they believed. The Chronos Corporation built the most advanced time machine ever conceived: **The Jolt Engine**. Capable of sending consciousness back through time while maintaining physical form in the present.

Dr. Elena Vance, lead researcher at Chronos, was the first to notice the anomalies. Ripples in the timestream. Historical artifacts appearing in places they shouldn't exist. The timeline was fragmenting.

## The Temporal Crisis

The Jolt Engine's first major test run created an unforeseen consequence: a **Temporal Shockwave** that scattered fragments of historical artifacts across all of human history. Instead of traveling to observe history, the engine had begun to *rewrite* it.

The artifacts—called **Chrono Fragments**—possessed immense time energy. They were drawn to specific moments in history, binding themselves to key events, figures, and locations. Now, without them, history itself was beginning to unravel.

## The Player's Role

You are a **Temporal Agent**—one of the few individuals whose consciousness is compatible with the Jolt Engine. Your mission: travel through time, locate the scattered Chrono Fragments, and restore the timeline before the anomalies cascade into a complete temporal collapse.

But the task is not simple. Each era you visit has been affected by the shockwave differently. Historical figures may be displaced. Events may be altered. You must navigate these changes while collecting fragments and understanding how to return them to their proper places.

## The World: Past Meets Future

Chronos technology has merged with historical environments, creating a unique aesthetic:

### The Time Hub
Your base of operations. A floating station in the space-time continuum where the Chrono Engine is housed. From here, you can:
- View your collection of Chrono Fragments
- Access the Temporal Map to plan expeditions
- Upgrade your Time Suit and equipment
- Receive missions from Dr. Vance
- Trade with other Temporal Agents

### Historical Eras

#### Mesopotamia — The Dawn of Civilization (3500 BCE)
The first era affected. The ziggurats glow with temporal energy. Cuneiform tablets display equations that shouldn't exist for centuries. The Code of Hammurabi has clauses about time travel—written by agents who've traveled here before.

**Environment:** Desert sands meet holographic displays. Ancient temples have energy conduits. Camels drink from oasis water that sparkles with temporal particles.

#### Ancient Egypt — Empire of Eternity (2500 BCE)
The pyramid complex now pulses with blue-white light. hieroglyphics include diagrams of the Chrono Engine. The Sphinx's eyes glow with the same cyan that powers your Time Suit.

**Environment:** Sand-colored architecture with glowing blue accents. The Nile flows with water that shimmers like liquid glass. Obelisks emit soft temporal vibrations.

#### Ancient Greece — The Age of Reason (500 BCE)
The Parthenon houses a miniature Jolt Engine. Philosophers debate the nature of time with foreknowledge they shouldn't possess. The Oracle at Delphi speaks of futures yet to come.

**Environment:** Marble white structures with cyan energy threading through columns. The Acropolis floats slightly above ground level—temporal displacement.

#### Roman Empire — The Eternal City (100 CE)
Rome has been retrofitted with Chronos technology. Aqueducts now glow with temporal energy. The Colosseum hosts games that include temporal obstacles. Emperors consult holographic advisors.

**Environment:** Roman concrete mixed with glowing energy conduits. Forges produce Time Suit components. Centurions wear armor with temporal shielding.

#### Medieval Europe — The Dark Ages (1200 CE)
Castle walls contain temporal stabilizers. Cathedral spires emit soft glows. The Black Knight's armor includes pieces of Time Suit technology from the future.

**Environment:** Stone castles with glowing runes. Torches burn with cyan-tinted flames. Dragons are revealed as temporal creatures escaped from other eras.

#### Renaissance — The Rebirth (1500 CE)
Da Vinci has created sketches of time machines centuries ahead of their time. Michelangelo's sculptures occasionally shimmer and phase. The Medici banking system includes accounts from the 22nd century.

**Environment:** Renaissance art mixed with floating holographic displays. Canals glow with temporal reflections. Airships drift above Florence.

## The Chrono Fragments

Each fragment is a piece of temporal history:

### Fragment Types

| Type | Color | Power | Rarity |
|------|-------|-------|--------|
| **Kinetic** | Cyan (#00D9FF) | Speed, agility | Common |
| **Shield** | Teal (#00FFE5) | Defense, protection | Uncommon |
| **Power** | Gold (#FFD700) | Strength, abilities | Rare |
| **Mystery** | Purple (#9D4EDD) | Unknown potential | Legendary |

### Fragment Collection
- Fragments bind to specific historical moments
- Each era contains exactly 10 fragments
- Collecting fragments powers your Time Suit
- Full sets unlock era-specific abilities
- Collecting all fragments in an era unlocks the next

## The Antagonists

### The Fractured
Agents who went mad from temporal exposure. They believe the timeline should remain fractured—that chaos is freedom. They actively collect fragments to prevent restoration.

### The Silence
A rogue AI from the future that wants to end all timelines. It sends agents back to sabotage the Jolt Engine and ensure temporal collapse.

### Temporal Anomalies
Wild temporal creatures born from the shockwave. They wander through time, disrupting events. Some can be captured and tamed.

## Time Energy System

All progression is powered by **Time Energy**:
- Completing missions generates Time Energy
- Collecting fragments converts to Time Energy
- Time Energy upgrades your Time Suit
- Time Energy powers temporal abilities

**Rules:**
- Time Energy never decreases
- All upgrades are permanent
- No real-money purchase of Time Energy
- Progression is 100% gameplay-based

## The Timeline Restoration

Your ultimate goal: restore all 60 fragments (6 eras × 10 fragments) and complete the timeline restoration. Upon completion:
- The Temporal Shockwave is undone
- History returns to normal
- The Jolt Engine is stabilized
- You become a Guardian of Time

But the story doesn't end there. Future updates will introduce new threats, new eras, and new ways to explore humanity's past.
