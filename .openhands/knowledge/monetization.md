# Jolt Time — Monetization Strategy

## Core Revenue Principle

> **Jolt Time is NOT a Play-To-Earn game. Players never earn real money. This is strictly a free-to-play educational entertainment product with ethical monetization.**

The primary revenue source is advertising through **AdsGram**. All monetization decisions are evaluated against the core promise of fair, non-predatory player experience.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Primary: AdsGram Advertisements

**Why AdsGram:**
- Telegram-native advertising platform
- Non-intrusive ad formats
- Fair revenue share for developers
- Built-in targeting capabilities
- Compliant with Telegram policies

**Ad Formats:**

| Format | Placement | Frequency | Duration |
|--------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Rewarded Video | Bonus rewards, continue playing | Player-initiated | 15-30 sec |
| Interstitial | Level completion, loading screens | Max 1 per 3 min | 5 sec non-skippable |
| Banner | Persistent bottom bar | Always visible | Persistent |
| Playable Ads | Cross-promotion | As needed | Interactive demo |

**Implementation Guidelines:**
- Never force ads — always player choice for rewarded ads
- Banner ads should not obstruct gameplay
- Interstitials only on natural breaks
- Clear labeling of sponsored content
- Respect user's "hide ads" preference if offered

**Revenue Estimation (per 1000 DAU):**
- Rewarded videos: 3-5 RPM
- Interstitials: 1-2 RPM
- Banners: 0.5-1 RPM
- Blended: $2-5 CPM

### 2. Secondary: Cosmetic Purchases

**Philosophy:**
- All purchases are purely cosmetic
- No gameplay advantage for paying players
- No "pay-to-win" mechanics
- Equality of experience for all players

**Cosmetic Categories:**

| Category | Items | Price Range |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Time Wanderer Skins | Outfits, accessories | $1.99 - $4.99 |
| Era Themes | Visual themes per civilization | $0.99 - $2.99 |
| Shard Auras | Visual effects for collections | $0.99 - $3.99 |
| Chat Stickers | In-game emotes | $0.49 - $1.99 |
| Profile Frames | Profile picture borders | $0.99 - $1.99 |

**Purchase Principles:**
- Clear display of what is purchased
- Preview before purchase
- One-time purchase (no subscriptions)
- Restore purchases functionality
- Regional pricing adjustments

### 3. Tertiary: Battle Pass / Season Pass

**Season Structure:**
- 8-week seasons aligned with content updates
- Free track: 15 tiers of rewards
- Premium track ($3.99): 30 tiers of rewards
- Premium includes exclusive cosmetics + bonus XP

**Premium Track Contents:**
- Exclusive Time Wanderer skin
- Era theme pack
- Shard aura
- Profile badge
- 2x XP boost for season duration

**Ethics:**
- Free players can still progress
- Premium is cosmetic + convenience only
- No FOMO mechanics
- Season content available forever (not exclusive)

### 4. Future: Telegram Mini App Store

**Opportunity:**
- Telegram is developing its own app store
- Jolt Time positioned for featured placement
- Premium placement requires quality standards
- Revenue share TBD by Telegram

**Preparation:**
- Maintain Telegram compliance
- High rating on user reviews
- Regular update cadence
- Feature-complete product

### Revenue Model Validation

- **No gambling mechanics** — No loot boxes, gacha, or randomized paid items
- **No artificial scarcity** — Items available for reasonable time
- **No social pressure** — No "pay or your friends suffer" mechanics
- **No misleading pricing** — Clear, honest pricing

## Player Value Optimization

### Lifetime Value Drivers

1. **Engagement** — More playtime = more ad impressions = higher LTV
2. **Retention** — Longer retention = more sessions = higher LTV
3. **Social** — More friends = more sharing = organic growth = lower CAC
4. **Satisfaction** — Happy players = positive reviews = lower CAC

## Ethical Commitments

### We Will Never:
- Sell gameplay advantages for money
- Use artificial urgency or FOMO
- Implement loot boxes or gambling mechanics
- Make promises about earning real money
- Target vulnerable players with predatory offers
- Hide the true cost of any purchase

### We Will Always:
- Clearly label sponsored content
- Respect player time
- Provide fair value for purchases
- Allow complete f2p experience
- Be transparent about monetization
- Listen to player feedback
