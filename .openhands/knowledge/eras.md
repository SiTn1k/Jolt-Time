# Jolt Time — Historical Eras

## Overview

Jolt Time spans seven major historical eras, each with unique atmosphere, visual identity, and artifact themes. Every era is designed to be historically authentic while incorporating the game's temporal sci-fi elements. The game serves as an educational journey through human history.

---
