# Jolt Time — Marketplace System

## Overview

The Jolt Time Marketplace is a player-to-player (PvP) trading system that enables artifact collectors, cosmetic enthusiasts, and economy-minded players to buy, sell, and trade in-game items. The marketplace strengthens the overall economy by providing legitimate channels for item exchange while protecting against abuse, duplication, and artificial inflation.

**Core Philosophy:** Trading is completely optional. Players who enjoy collecting can collect; players who enjoy economy management can trade. The marketplace never forces participation and must never undermine AdsGram's role as the primary revenue system.

## 1. Marketplace Categories

The marketplace organizes items into five distinct categories, each serving different player interests.

### Artifact Market

The Artifact Market is the primary trading hub for historical artifacts across all eras:

- **Available Items:** All artifacts from Mesopotamia, Egypt, Greece, Rome, and future eras
- **Rarity Spectrum:** Common through Legendary, including evolved and enhanced artifacts
- **Era-Specific Listings:** Filter by era for focused collection building
- **Set Completion Trading:** Special sub-section for artifacts needed to complete collections

### Cosmetic Market

The Cosmetic Market handles all visual and decorative items:

- **Profile Frames:** Decorative borders for player profiles
- **Titles:** Achievement-based and special event titles
- **Badges:** Achievement badges and event participation badges
- **Banners:** Guild and personal display banners
- **Chat Stickers:** In-chat expressive stickers
- **Avatar Frames:** Special profile picture decorations

### Event Collectibles

The Event Collectibles section trades limited-time event items:

- **Seasonal Artifacts:** Artifacts exclusive to specific events
- **Event Cosmetics:** Skins, frames, and badges from past events
- **Anniversary Items:** Special commemorative pieces
- **Collaboration Items:** Cross-promotional collectibles (when applicable)
- **Trading Windows:** Event items may only be tradeable during specific periods or after event conclusion

### Museum Decorations

The Museum Decorations category trades museum-related items:

- **Display Cases:** Specialized artifact display furniture
- **Exhibit Themes:** Era-specific museum styling packs
- **Lighting Options:** Ambient lighting for museum rooms
- **Background Scenes:** Historical scene backdrop items
- **Placards and Labels:** Customizable museum signage
- **Rare Exhibits:** Limited display items for prestigious collections

### Limited Items

The Limited Items section features the rarest and most exclusive offerings:

- **Rare Drops:** Items with extremely low acquisition rates
- **Promotional Items:** Launch event and special promotion exclusive items
- **Prototype Artifacts:** Special variant items with unique aesthetics
- **Mint Condition Pieces:** Pristine versions of otherwise common items
- **One-of-One Items:** Truly unique items with special designations

## 2. Trading Philosophy

The trading philosophy establishes clear principles that guide all marketplace decisions.

### Player Empowerment

Players should be able to:

- **Buy Items:** Acquire artifacts, cosmetics, and collectibles from other players
- **Sell Items:** Convert unwanted inventory into usable currency
- **Collect Rare Artifacts:** Build prestigious collections through marketplace trading
- **Participate in the Economy:** Engage with the economic systems without mandatory participation

### Optional Participation

**Trading must remain completely optional.** No gameplay progression, story completion, or meaningful game state should require marketplace participation. Players who never trade should experience zero disadvantage.

### Economy Strengthening

The marketplace strengthens the overall economy by:

- Providing legitimate channels for item exchange
- Creating additional sinks for Chrono Coins and Event Tokens
- Enabling collectors to complete sets without pure luck
- Generating marketplace fees that support game development
- Encouraging long-term player engagement through collection goals

### Anti-Griefing Principles

- No forced sales or buyouts
- Price manipulation protections
-冷却 periods for controversial items
- Community reporting systems for abusive listings

## 4. Listing System

The listing system defines how items enter and exit the marketplace.

### Listing Duration

Items can be listed for the following durations:

| Duration | Use Case | Extension Allowed |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| 24 hours | Quick sales, test pricing | Yes (once) |
| 3 days | Standard listings | Yes (once) |
| 7 days | Premium items | Yes (once) |
| 14 days | Rare collectibles | Yes (once) |

### Item Price

Sellers set their own prices with the following guidelines:

- **Minimum Price:** 1 Chrono Coin (prevents free item abuse)
- **Maximum Price:** Configurable per rarity tier to prevent exploitation
- **Price History:** Players can view recent sale prices for reference
- **Price Suggestions:** System provides pricing guidance based on market data

### Item Quantity

- **Single Listings:** One item per listing (default for unique items)
- **Multi-Quantity Listings:** Stackable items (artifacts of same type) allow quantity selection
- **Maximum Per Listing:** Varies by item type and rarity

### Expiration Rules

- **Manual Removal:** Sellers can remove listings anytime before sale
- **Duration Expiration:** Unpurchased items return to seller inventory automatically
- **No-Refresh Penalty:** Listings that expire without sale count against seller limits
- **Notification at Expiration:** Players notified when listings expire

## 8. Marketplace Protection

Comprehensive protections maintain marketplace integrity and player trust.

### Duplicate Item Prevention

- **Ownership Verification:** Items must be in seller inventory before listing
- **Listing Lock:** Items cannot be listed while in another active listing
- **Transfer Confirmation:** Two-step confirmation for high-value items
- **Duplicate Detection:** Algorithm identifies and flags potential duplicates
- **One-Item-One-Sale:** Each item instance can only be sold once

### Suspicious Transaction Detection

- **Price Anomaly Detection:** Flags unusually high or low prices
- **Rapid Trading Patterns:** Identifies wash trading attempts
- **Account Linking Detection:** Prevents coordinated price manipulation
- **Volume Analysis:** Monitors for pump-and-dump schemes
- **Automatic Investigation:** Flags trigger manual review process

### Exploit Abuse Prevention

- **Listing Exploits:** Prevents rapid relisting to bypass fees
- **Currency Exploits:** Detects abnormal currency movements
- **Inventory Manipulation:** Prevents items from being created or destroyed
- **Race Condition Protection:** Ensures atomic transactions for all sales
- **Rollback Capabilities:** Ability to reverse fraudulent transactions

### Artificial Inflation Control

- **Price Ceilings:** Maximum prices per item rarity tier
- **Marketplace Cooling:** Limits on how quickly items can be relisted
- **Supply Monitoring:** Tracks total item supply across marketplace
- **Demand Analysis:** Identifies artificial demand patterns
- **Anti-Manipulation Algorithms:** Machine learning detection of price manipulation

## 10. Marketplace Fees

Marketplace fees serve economic stabilization purposes and are documented philosophically.

### Fee Structure Philosophy

Fees should:

- **Prevent Abuse:** Discourage excessive listings and relisting
- **Support Development:** Provide sustainable revenue for project
- **Maintain Balance:** Not so high as to kill trading activity
- **Scale with Value:** Higher-value items pay higher fees
- **Reward Loyalty:** Lower fees for established players (future feature)

### Fee Destinations

- **Development Fund:** Primary recipient, supports ongoing development
- **Prize Pool:** Percentage to tournament and event prize pools
- **Community Fund:** Supports community events and initiatives
- **Operational Costs:** Covers payment processing and infrastructure

### Fee Calculations

- **Standard Fee:** Percentage of sale price
- **Duration Fee:** Additional fee for longer listing periods
- **Featured Listing Fee:** Optional fee for prominent placement
- **Relisting Fee:** Discounted fee for re-listing unsold items

### Fee Transparency

- All fees clearly displayed before listing confirmation
- Fee breakdown shown at point of sale
- Fee history visible in seller dashboard
- No hidden fees or surprise charges

- Item name and rarity

- Link to transaction details

**Frequency:** Immediate upon sale completion

### Completed Purchase Notifications

**Trigger:** Player successfully purchases an item

- Item name and rarity

- Link to view purchased item

**Frequency:** Immediate upon purchase completion

### Expired Listing Notifications

**Trigger:** A listing reaches its end date without sale

- Item name and rarity
- Original listing price
- Days listed
- Option to relist with same or adjusted pricing

**Frequency:** One notification per expired listing

### Price Change Notifications (Optional)

**Trigger:** Items on player's wishlist change price

- Item name and new price
- Price change direction
- Option to purchase immediately

**Frequency:** As changes occur, respects player preferences

### Notification Guidelines

**Never become spam.** Strict controls prevent notification fatigue:

- Maximum 3 marketplace notifications per day per player
- Notification preferences fully customizable
- Quiet hours respected
- Batch summary option instead of individual notifications
- One-click unsubscribe from all marketplace notifications

## 12. AdsGram Integration

The marketplace must never replace AdsGram as the primary revenue system.

### Revenue System Hierarchy

1. **AdsGram (Primary):** Main revenue source for project sustainability
2. **Marketplace Fees (Secondary):** Additional revenue from player trading
3. **Optional Purchases (Tertiary):** Cosmetic items and convenience features

### Marketplace and AdsGram Separation

- **No Ads in Marketplace:** Marketplace interface remains ad-free
- **No Marketplace Rewards for Ads:** Ads do not grant marketplace bonuses
- **Independent Systems:** Marketplace operates without impacting AdsGram engagement

### Optional AdsGram Rewards for Marketplace

AdsGram may optionally provide marketplace-related bonuses:

- **Additional Listing Slots:** Extra simultaneous listings for engaged players
- **Temporary Marketplace Bonuses:** Reduced fees for limited periods
- **Featured Listing Opportunities:** Promotional placement without payment
- **Marketplace Boost:** Enhanced visibility for listed items

### Critical Principle

**Ads must always remain completely optional.** No gameplay advantage, progression requirement, or meaningful benefit can be contingent on watching ads. Marketplace bonuses from AdsGram engagement are purely additive conveniences.
