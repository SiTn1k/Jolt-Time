# Jolt Time — Artifact Collections System

## Overview

The Artifact Collections System is the crown jewel of Jolt Time's long-term engagement strategy. It transforms artifact hunting from a passive activity into a deliberate, rewarding pursuit that gives players meaningful goals spanning months and years. Every collection is a journey through history—complete sets to unlock the secrets of civilizations, discover hidden combinations to reveal secret collections, and showcase your mastery to the world.

This system is designed to **increase retention without becoming mandatory**. Players who engage with collections feel rewarded; players who prefer other activities are never locked out of content or penalized for not collecting.

| Motivation Type | What They Seek | Collections That Appeal |
|-----------------|----------------|------------------------|
| **Completionist** | 100% collection | Full era sets, all historical sets |
| **Rarity Hunter** | Legendary/Mythic finds | Special rarity collections |
| **History Enthusiast** | Learning through play | Civilization collections |
| **Social Collector** | Recognition and display | Showcase collections, rare displays |
| **Discovery Seeker** | Hidden secrets | Secret collections, rare combinations |
| **Power Builder** | Combat effectiveness | Set bonuses, synergy collections |

### Design Rules

```
✅ Collections increase retention without becoming mandatory
✅ Progress is always visible and celebrated
✅ Rare artifacts feel special, not required
✅ Hidden content rewards curiosity, not grinding
✅ Set bonuses are cosmetic and convenience, never pay-to-win
✅ Every collection tells a historical story
✅ Completion feels like scholarly achievement
✅ No collection is an island—cross-era connections exist

❌ No FOMO-driven collection pressure
❌ No collection that gates gameplay progression
❌ No artificial scarcity forcing purchases
❌ No hidden collections requiring unreasonable actions
❌ No collections that feel like homework
```

## 4. Partial Completion Rewards

Players should never feel that incomplete collections are wasted progress. **Partial completion is always rewarded.**

### Partial Completion Milestones

| Progress | Reward | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **25%** | Minor reward | First taste of completion |
| **50%** | Moderate reward | Halfway celebration |
| **75%** | Substantial reward | Final push motivation |
| **100%** | Full reward | Complete celebration |

### Milestone Reward Structure

```
PARTIAL COMPLETION REWARDS:

25% MILESTONE — "First Steps"
├── XP: 50 (scaled by collection size)
├── Dust: 25 (scaled by collection size)
├── Badge: Collection-specific bronze badge
└── UI: Progress bar turns green (started)

50% MILESTONE — "Halfway There"
├── XP: 100
├── Dust: 50
├── Badge: Collection-specific silver badge
├── Preview: Show set bonus preview in UI
└── UI: Progress bar shows completion preview

75% MILESTONE — "Almost Complete"
├── XP: 200
├── Dust: 100
├── Frame: Collection-themed frame (unlocked, not equipped)
├── Notification: "One set away from completion!"
└── UI: Missing artifacts highlighted in collection view

100% MILESTONE — "Collection Complete!"
├── XP: 500
├── Dust: 250
├── Full Reward: Set bonus unlocked
├── Celebration: Animated completion screen
├── Badge: Gold badge added to profile
└── UI: Collection marked as complete, shows completion date

### Missing Artifact Hints

At 75% completion, players receive contextual hints about remaining artifacts:

```
75% HINT SYSTEM:
├── Era-based hints: "This artifact is from [Era Name]"
├── Rarity hints: "The remaining artifact is [Rarity]"
├── Type hints: "You need a [Type] artifact"

## 6. Secret Collections

Secret Collections add an element of discovery and surprise to Jolt Time. They reward curious players who explore various aspects of the game naturally.

### Secret Collection Philosophy

```
SECRET COLLECTION DESIGN RULES:
✅ Players discover them naturally through gameplay
✅ No obscure actions or grinding required
✅ Hints appear when player is close to discovery
✅ Discovery feels like a "aha!" moment
✅ No secret collection is required for any achievement
✅ Secrets add value, not anxiety

SECRET COLLECTION DISCOVERY:
├── 0% known: Shows as "???" with vague hint
├── 25% known: Partial hint reveals era/timeline
├── 50% known: Type hint revealed
├── 75% known: Discovery notification appears
└── 100% known: Collection revealed and celebrated
```

### Showcase Philosophy

```
SHOWCASE PRINCIPLES:
✅ Players choose what to display
✅ Showcases are always visible to others
✅ Rare displays confer social prestige
✅ Multiple showcase slots for variety
✅ Showcase updates are always optional
✅ No showcase = profile still looks good
```
