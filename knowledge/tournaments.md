# Tournament and Championship System

## Overview

The Tournament System transforms Jolt Time's competitive scene into a layered ecosystem of events, from daily skirmishes to legendary championship battles. Tournaments create memorable moments, celebrate skill and dedication, and provide additional pathways to recognition and rewards. Every competitive system in Jolt Time is designed to be fair, accessible, and completely free from pay-to-win mechanics—victory belongs to the skilled, not to those who spend the most.

---

## 1. Tournament Categories

### Daily Tournaments

**Purpose:** Quick competitive events for daily engagement.

**Characteristics:**
- Available every day
- Single-elimination format
- Quick matches (15-30 minutes)
- Entry open for limited windows
- Lower-tier rewards but accessible

**Structure:**
```
Entry Window: 12:00 - 20:00 UTC
Tournament Start: 20:00 UTC
Duration: 30-60 minutes
```

**Rewards:**
- Participation badge
- 50-100 Chrono Coins
- Tournament points for weekly standing

**Requirements:**
- Player Level 5+
- 1 Tournament Ticket (granted daily free)
- 1 hour minimum play time today

---

### Weekly Tournaments

**Purpose:** Mid-tier competitive events with better rewards.

**Characteristics:**
- Available every week (Saturday-Sunday)
- Double-elimination format
- Longer matches and more rounds
- Better rewards than daily
- Builds toward monthly standing

**Structure:**
```
Entry Window: Saturday 00:00 - Sunday 12:00 UTC
Tournament Start: Sunday 14:00 UTC
Duration: 2-4 hours
```

**Rewards:**
- Weekly Trophy Badge
- 200-500 Chrono Coins
- Epic Fragment
- Weekly Standing Points

**Requirements:**
- Player Level 10+
- 3 Tournament Tickets
- Diamond league or below (top leagues excluded)

---

### Seasonal Championships

**Purpose:** Major competitive events marking season milestones.

**Characteristics:**
- Held at end of each season (aligned with Battle Pass)
- Multi-day tournament format
- Group stage + knockout rounds
- Premium rewards and exclusive titles
- Community-wide viewership

**Structure:**
```
Registration: Week before season end
Group Stage: Season End Week (Days 1-3)
Knockout: Season End Week (Days 4-5)
Finals: Day 6 (Live Event)
```

**Rewards:**
- Seasonal Champion Badge
- Exclusive Frame
- 1000-5000 Chrono Coins
- Title: "[Season Name] Champion"

**Requirements:**
- Player Level 20+
- Top 100 in seasonal rankings
- Invitation required

---

### Community Tournaments

**Purpose:** Player-organized competitive events.

**Characteristics:**
- Created by players or community managers
- Customizable rules (within guidelines)
- Various formats and sizes
- Community engagement events
- Creator-sponsored prizes

**Tournament Sizes:**
| Size | Participants | Duration | Prize Pool |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|

| Medium | 32-64 | 2-4 hours | Coins + badges |
| Large | 128-256 | Half-day | Frames + coins |
| Community Event | 512+ | Full day | Rare cosmetics |

**Requirements:**

- No entry fees (free tournaments only)
- Fair play agreement required

---

### Invitational Events

**Purpose:** Elite competitions featuring top players.

**Characteristics:**

- Special formats and rules
- Enhanced prestige
- Special broadcasting/streaming
- Exclusive rewards

## 2. Tournament Formats

### Single Elimination

**Structure:** One loss and you're out.

```
Round of 16 → Quarterfinals → Semifinals → Finals → Champion
```

**Pros:**
- Simple to understand
- Quick tournament completion
- High stakes every match

**Cons:**
- One bad day ends your run
- Less competitive for lower placements

**Best For:** Daily tournaments, beginner-friendly events

---

### Double Elimination

**Structure:** Two losses to be eliminated.

```
Winners Bracket                    Losers Bracket
    │                                  │
    ▼                                  ▼
Round 1                              
    │                                  │
    ▼                                  ▼
Winners Round 2        ───────►      Losers Round 1
    │                                  │
    ▼                                  ▼
Winners Quarters      ───────►      Losers Quarters
    │                                  │
    ▼                                  ▼
Winners Semis         ───────►      Losers Semis
    │                                  │
    ▼                                  ▼
Winners Finals         ───────►      Losers Finals
        │                              │
        │◄────────────┐               │
        ▼             │               ▼
   Grand Finals    ◄───┘          3rd Place
        │
        ▼
   CHAMPION
```

**Pros:**
- More forgiving for skilled players
- True second-place battle
- More matches = more learning

**Cons:**
- Longer duration
- More complex scheduling

**Best For:** Weekly tournaments, championship events

---

### Group Stage + Knockout

**Structure:** Initial group round, then top advance to knockout.

```
GROUP STAGE                          KNOCKOUT
    │                                 │
    ▼                                 ▼
Group A: 8 players            Quarterfinals (Top 2 from each group)
Group B: 8 players                   │
Group C: 8 players                   ▼
Group D: 8 players              Semifinals
    │                                 │
    ▼                                 ▼
Top 2 from each group          Finals
(8 players)                          │
                                    ▼
                               CHAMPION
```

**Pros:**
- Multiple matches against different opponents
- Bad day doesn't end tournament
- Groups reveal true skill over time

**Cons:**
- Complex scheduling
- Longer overall duration

**Best For:** Seasonal championships, large invitationals

---

### League Format (Future)

**Concept:** Round-robin competition over extended period.

**Structure:**
- All participants play each other
- Points determine standings
- Top advance to finals

**Requirements:**
- Significant time commitment
- Large administrative overhead
- Scheduling infrastructure

**Best For:** Esports leagues, regional championships

## 3. Entry Philosophy

### Entry Pathways

**Normal Participation Entry:**
- Complete daily missions
- Achieve ranked match wins
- Participate in community events
- Simply play the game actively

**Seasonal Qualification:**
- Reach top 100 in seasonal rankings
- Earn invitation through performance
- Maintain activity requirements
- Demonstrate consistent skill

**Ranking Requirements:**
| Tournament | Minimum League | Additional |
|------------|----------------|------------|
| Daily | Any | Level 5+ |
| Weekly | Bronze+ | Level 10+ |
| Seasonal Championship | Gold+ | Top 100 seasonal |
| Invitational | Master+ | By invitation |

### Entry Fairness

**Principles:**
- No pay-to-enter (always free)
- No advantage for spending
- Skill-based matchmaking
- Fair brackets based on ranking

**Anti-Abuse:**
- One entry per player per tournament
- Smurf detection
- Account linking requirements
- Fair play agreements

```
🏆 Jolt Time Tournament

[Event Name]

```

## 10. AdsGram Integration

### Tournament Mode and AdsGram

AdsGram remains the primary revenue system. Tournament content respects this:

**Optional Rewards (Never Required):**
- Bonus tournament tickets (extra entries)
- Additional daily entries
- Temporary seasonal XP boost
- Skip wait timers (if any)

**Never Included in Tournaments:**
- No required watching for access
- No competitive advantage
- No better brackets or byes
- No exclusive tournament content

### Ad Examples for Tournament Mode

```
"Watch an ad to earn an extra Tournament Ticket"
"Watch to boost your seasonal tournament standing by 10%"
"Watch to claim missed daily tournament rewards"
```

### Ad Respect Guidelines

- Maximum 1 rewarded ad per tournament day
- Clear labeling of optional nature
- Easy dismiss option
- No artificial urgency

## 15. Tournament Management

### Anti-Abuse Measures

**Account Integrity:**
- Phone verification for tournament entry
- Device fingerprinting
- IP monitoring for multi-accounting
- Smurf detection algorithms

**Match Integrity:**
- Anti-cheat systems
- Automated suspicious behavior detection
- Manual review for flagged matches
- Leaver detection and penalties

### Penalties

| Violation | First Offense | Second Offense | Third Offense |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| Cheating | 7-day ban | 30-day ban | Permanent |
| Smurfing | Reset to new | 7-day ban | 30-day ban |
| Rage-quit | Warning | 1-day ban | 7-day ban |
| Abuse | Warning | 3-day ban | 30-day ban |
| Exploiting | 30-day ban | Permanent | — |
