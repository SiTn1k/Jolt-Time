# Guild System and Guild Wars

## Overview

The Guild System transforms Jolt Time into a community experience where players unite under shared banners, collaborate on missions, and compete in seasonal guild wars. Guilds create long-term communities that encourage cooperation, celebrate collective achievements, and foster healthy competition. All guild systems are designed to be fair, inclusive, and never pay-to-win.

---

## 1. Guild Creation Rules

### Basic Requirements

**Minimum Requirements to Create:**
- Player Level 10 or higher
- No existing guild membership
- 500 Chrono Coins (founder fee)
- Unique guild name (not taken)
- Telegram account in good standing

**Guild Name Rules:**
- 3-24 characters
- Alphanumeric and spaces only
- No special characters or emojis in name
- No offensive or copyrighted terms
- Must be unique across the platform

**Guild Description:**
- Optional, 0-200 characters
- Describes guild purpose or identity
- Can be updated by leadership

**Guild Icon:**
- Required upon creation
- Choose from preset icons OR
- Upload custom icon (future feature)
- Icons must be appropriate (no offensive content)

### Guild Size Limits

| Guild Level | Member Limit | Officer Limit |
|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | 10 | 1 |
| 2 | 15 | 2 |
| 3 | 25 | 3 |
| 4 | 35 | 4 |
| 5 | 50 | 5 |
| 6 | 75 | 6 |
| 7 | 100 | 8 |

**Invite System:**
- Members can be invited by Leaders/Officers
- Players accept invitation to join
- No open enrollment (invite-only)
- Players can only belong to one guild

### Guild Privacy

**Public Guilds:**
- Visible in guild directory
- Can receive applications
- Listed on leaderboards

**Private Guilds:**
- Not visible in directory
- Invite-only only
- Hidden from leaderboards

## 2. Guild Roles and permissions

### Role Hierarchy

```
GUILD LEADER
    │
    ├── GUILD OFFICERS (up to guild level limit)
    │       │
    │       └── GUILD MEMBERS
```

### Role Permissions

| Permission | Leader | Officer | Member |
|------------|--------|---------|--------|
| Edit Guild Name/Description | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Change Guild Icon | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Kick Members | ✓ | ✓ (non-officers) | ✗ |
| Promote to Officer | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Demote from Officer | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Transfer Leadership | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Invite New Members | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| Accept Applications | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| Create Guild Missions | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| Start Guild Wars | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Manage War Strategy | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| View Guild Statistics | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Participate in Missions | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Donate Resources | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Chat in Guild Channel | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

### Leader-Specific Powers

- **Transfer Leadership:** Assign another member as new leader
- **Disband Guild:** Permanently delete guild (all progress lost)
- **Promote Officer:** Elevate member to officer status
- **Demote Officer:** Remove officer privileges
- **Override Decisions:** Final say in all guild matters

### Officer-Specific Powers

- **Invite Members:** Add new players to guild
- **Kick Non-Officers:** Remove regular members
- **Manage Missions:** Create and monitor guild missions
- **War Coordination:** Help leader with battle strategy

### Member Privileges

- **Participate Fully:** Join missions and wars
- **Donate:** Contribute resources to guild
- **Chat:** Participate in guild communication
- **Vote:** Participate in guild polls (if enabled)
- **View Stats:** See guild statistics and progress

## 4. Guild Progression

### Guild XP System

**Earning Guild XP:**

| Activity | XP Earned | Per What |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Member Level Up | 10 × player level | Per level up |
| Daily Login (member) | 5 XP | Per member |
| Mission Complete | 50-200 XP | Per mission |
| War Win | 500 XP | Per victory |
| War Participation | 100 XP | Per battle |
| Achievement Unlocked | 25-100 XP | Per achievement |
| Season Event | 200-500 XP | Per event |

**Guild Leveling Table:**

| Level | Total XP Required | Member Limit | Officer Limit | New Benefit |
|-------|------------------|--------------|---------------|-------------|
| 1 | 0 | 10 | 1 | Base |
| 2 | 1,000 | 15 | 2 | +5 members |
| 3 | 3,000 | 25 | 3 | +10 members |
| 4 | 7,500 | 35 | 4 | +10 members |
| 5 | 15,000 | 50 | 5 | +15 members |
| 6 | 30,000 | 75 | 6 | +25 members |
| 7 | 60,000 | 100 | 8 | Max capacity |

### Progression Rewards

**Guild-Level Rewards (Cosmetic Only):**
- Guild Banner designs unlocked at levels 2, 4, 6
- Guild Emblem frames unlocked at levels 3, 5, 7
- Guild Achievement badges for milestones
- Guild Title for members ("Guild Name" Member)

**Individual Rewards (Cosmetic Only):**
- Member badges showing guild level
- Profile frames with guild colors
- Titles based on guild achievements
- Guild-specific chat effects

### Progression Philosophy

**Cosmetic Focus:**
- All guild progression rewards are cosmetic
- No gameplay advantages from guild level
- Prestige and identity are primary value
- Community, not power, drives participation

## 5. Guild Missions

### Mission Types

**Daily Guild Missions:**
- Reset at UTC midnight
- 3 missions per day (shared by guild)
- All members contribute to completion
- Must be completed by all members for full reward

**Weekly Guild Missions:**
- Reset Monday at UTC midnight
- 5 missions per week
- Require sustained effort
- Higher rewards than daily

**Seasonal Guild Missions:**
- Tied to Battle Pass season
- 10 missions per season
- Contribute to guild season score
- Special cosmetic rewards

## 7. Guild Wars

### War Structure

**Seasonal Format:**
- Wars last 4 weeks (1 month season)
- Qualifier rounds first 3 weeks
- Finals in week 4
- War season aligns with Battle Pass season

**Matchmaking:**
- Guilds matched by guild level and season points
- Matching within same tier bracket
- No pay-to-win matchmaking advantage
- Fair competition based on participation

### War Mechanics

**Battle System:**
- Each war consists of multiple battles
- Each battle is a best-of-3 match format
- Guild members contribute battle points
- Higher participation = higher team score

**War Phases:**
```
Week 1-3: Qualifier Rounds
    │
    ├── 5 wars per week
    ├── Top guilds advance
    └── Bottom guilds get participation rewards

Week 4: Championship Finals
    │
    ├── Top 16 guilds compete
    ├── Single elimination bracket
    └── Winner gets ultimate prize
```

**War Participation Requirements:**
- Minimum 50% of active members must battle
- Each member limited to 10 battles per war
- Fair play enforcement (no cheating)
- Anti-smurfing (level requirements)

### War Scoring

**Point System:**
| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Battle Victory | 100 points |
| Battle Defeat | 25 points |
| Perfect Battle (no damage) | +50 bonus |
| Most Valuable Player | +25 bonus |
| Team Participation Bonus | 500 points (if >80% members) |

**Guild War Tiers:**
- Bronze (New guilds)
- Silver (Developing guilds)
- Gold (Established guilds)
- Platinum (Competitive guilds)
- Diamond (Top-tier guilds)
- Champion (Top 16 finalists)

### Guild War Rewards

**Season Placement Rewards:**

| Placement | Rewards |
|-----------|---------|
| 1st (Champion) | Trophy + Frame + Emote + Badge + 2000 Coins |
| 2nd (Finalist) | Trophy + Frame + Badge + 1500 Coins |
| 3rd-4th (Semifinal) | Trophy + Badge + 1000 Coins |
| 5th-16th | Badge + 500 Coins |
| All Participants | Badge + 250 Coins |

**Weekly Rewards:**
| Result | Rewards |
|--------|---------|
| War Win | 500 Guild XP, 100 Coins per member |
| War Loss | 200 Guild XP, 50 Coins per member |
| Perfect Week (all wins) | +250 Guild XP bonus |

### Fair Play in Guild Wars

**Anti-P2W Measures:**
- No advantage for spending money
- Matching based on participation, not power
- Level caps for competitive tiers
- All rewards are cosmetic or minor bonuses

**Anti-Cheating:**
- Automated cheat detection
- Manual review of suspicious activity
- Penalties for exploited bugs
- Report system for unfair play

Guild notifications keep members informed without spam:
- Maximum 3 guild notifications per week
- Only meaningful events trigger notifications
- Players control notification preferences
- Quiet hours respected

### Notification Triggers

**Membership Events:**
- Guild invitation received
- Application accepted/rejected
- Member joined/left
- Leadership transfer

**Guild Mission Events:**
- Daily mission available
- Mission progress milestone
- Mission completed

**War Events:**

## 11. AdsGram Integration

### Guild Mode and AdsGram

AdsGram remains the primary revenue system. Guild content respects this:

**Optional Rewards (Never Required):**
- Additional guild mission attempts
- Temporary activity boost (increased XP gain)
- Bonus guild rewards (one-time boosts)
- Skip wait timers (if any implemented)

**Never Included in Guild:**
- No required watching for guild access
- No competitive advantage for watching
- No exclusive guild content behind ads
- No pay-to-win elements

### Ad Examples for Guild Mode

```
"Watch an ad to boost guild mission progress by 25%"
"Watch to grant bonus activity to all guild members"
"Watch to claim missed daily guild rewards"
```

### Ad Respect Guidelines

- Maximum 2 rewarded ads per guild week
- Clear labeling of optional nature
- Easy dismiss option
- Guild leader can disable ads for guild

## 12. Community Philosophy

### Teamwork Principles

**Jolt Time Guilds Should:**
- Unite players with shared interests
- Encourage cooperation over competition
- Celebrate collective achievements
- Support newer members
- Foster long-term friendships

**Jolt Time Guilds Should NOT:**
- Require excessive time commitments
- Punish casual participation
- Foster toxic competition
- Exclude based on power level
- Create pay-to-win advantages

### Healthy Competition

**Guild Wars Should:**
- Reward participation over victory
- Provide fair matchmaking
- Celebrate improvement
- Maintain sportsmanship

**Guild Wars Should NOT:**
- Favor high-spenders
- Punish losing guilds harshly
- Create anxiety or stress
- Encourage toxic behavior

### Inclusive Design

**Accessibility:**
- Guilds work for all player levels
- Casual players feel welcome
- No mandatory daily requirements
- Rewards accessible to all members

**Diversity:**
- Different guild types welcomed
- Small guilds have own tier
- New guilds can grow
- No guild feels "left behind"

## 15. Member Experience

### Joining a Guild

**Discovery:**
1. Browse public guild directory
2. Filter by size, activity, language
3. View guild profiles
4. Submit application (if accepting)

**Invitation:**
1. Receive invite from guild
2. Review guild profile
3. Accept or decline
4. If accepted, join immediately

**Onboarding:**
- Welcome message from leader
- Guild rules/pinned info
- Introduction to missions
- War participation explained

### Active Membership

**Daily Experience:**
- Check guild missions
- Participate in progress
- Chat with members
- View war standings

**Weekly Experience:**
- War participation (if active)
- Mission completion rewards
- Leaderboard review
- Guild event participation

### Leaving a Guild

**Voluntary Leave:**
- Members can leave anytime
- 24-hour cooldown before joining another
- Progress with guild is retained
- Achievements stay with player

**Involuntary Leave (Kick):**
- Leader/officer can kick members
- Reason shown (optional)
- Appeal process available
- Rejoin cooldown applies
