# Battle Pass and Seasonal Progression System

## Overview

The Battle Pass system provides Jolt Time players with structured seasonal goals that reward consistent engagement. Each season spans approximately 10-12 weeks, offering both Free Track and Premium Track rewards. The system is designed to respect player time, avoid pay-to-win mechanics, and create meaningful progression milestones without forcing excessive grinding.

---

## 1. Battle Pass Structure

### Dual Track System

The Battle Pass consists of two parallel reward tracks that players progress through simultaneously:

**Free Track (Base Battle Pass)**
- Available to all players at no cost
- Provides essential progression rewards
- Ensures non-premium players remain competitive
- Cosmetic and practical rewards for casual engagement

**Premium Track (Enhanced Battle Pass)**
- Requires one-time purchase per season
- Unlocks exclusive cosmetic rewards
- Provides bonus progression conveniences
- Never grants combat advantages

### Track Progression

Both tracks share the same XP bar and level progression:
- Players earn XP through normal gameplay
- XP advances the shared Battle Pass level
- Reaching a level unlocks rewards on BOTH tracks
- Premium Track items are claimable; Free Track items are automatically received

```
Level 1  ─┬─ Free: 50 Chrono Coins
          └─ Premium: Basic Badge

Level 10 ─┬─ Free: Rare Fragment Pack
          └─ Premium: Era Frame

Level 20 ─┬─ Free: 200 Chrono Coins + 2x Artifact Boost
          └─ Premium: Exclusive Cosmetic

Level 30 ─┬─ Free: Epic Fragment
          └─ Premium: Premium Profile Frame

Level 50 ─┬─ Free: Legendary Fragment
          └─ Premium: Ultimate Cosmetic Bundle
```

### Season Duration

**Recommended Season Length: 10 Weeks (70 Days)**

This duration provides:
- Enough time for casual players to reach rewards
- Urgency without excessive pressure
- Regular content refresh cycles
- Buffer for life interruptions

**Season Timeline:**
```
Week 1-2:   Launch period — catching up, initial goals
Week 3-6:   Mid-season — steady progression, events
Week 7-9:   Final stretch — push to finish, limited events
Week 10:    Conclusion — season ends, transition prep
Week 11:    Transition — new season teaser, archive access
```

## 2. Season Structure

### Season Components

**1. Season Theme**
- Each season has a distinct historical focus
- Theme connects to era-specific rewards and cosmetics
- Narrative events tie into the season story

**2. Season Track**
- 50 levels total per season
- Each level requires increasing XP
- Free and Premium rewards at each level

**3. Season Events**
- 3-4 major events during the season
- Each event provides bonus XP
- Events offer unique limited-time rewards

**4. Season Challenges**
- Special objectives for bonus XP
- Challenge categories: Combat, Collection, Exploration
- Each challenge completed grants bonus rewards

### Reward Reset Philosophy

**What Resets Each Season:**
- Battle Pass level (starts at 1)
- Season-specific rewards
- Event progress
- Challenge progress

**What Persists Forever:**
- Lifetime statistics
- Overall player level
- Museum collection
- Permanent cosmetics (from past seasons)
- Story progress
- Achievement progress

**Legacy System:**
- Past season cosmetics become "Legacy" items
- Legacy items remain in player inventories
- Legacy items retain their cosmetic value
- Future legacy rewards possible for dedicated players

## 3. Battle Pass XP Sources

### Natural Progression Sources

Players should naturally gain XP through regular gameplay:

| Activity | XP per Action | Daily Cap | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-----------|-------|
| Daily Mission Complete | 50-200 | 3 missions | Difficulty-scaled |
| Weekly Mission Complete | 200-500 | 5 missions | Higher tier missions |
| Battle Victory | 25-100 | Unlimited | Scaled by opponent |
| Battle Defeat | 10-25 | Unlimited | Minimal but present |
| Artifact Collection | 10-50 | 5 artifacts | First discovery bonus |
| Era Exploration | 100-300 | 1 per era | Per major milestone |
| Story Chapter Complete | 500-1000 | As available | Major story beats |
| Boss Defeat (Normal) | 100-300 | Unlimited | Per boss |
| Boss Defeat (Elite) | 300-500 | Unlimited | Higher difficulty |
| Achievement Unlock | 50-500 | Unlimited | Tier-scaled |

### Event XP Sources

| Event Type | XP Bonus | Duration | Frequency |
|------------|----------|----------|-----------|
| Daily Event | 50-100 | 1 day | Daily |
| Weekly Event | 200-400 | 7 days | Weekly |
| Seasonal Event | 500-1000 | 3-7 days | 3-4 per season |
| Limited Challenge | 100-200 | 1-3 days | As available |

### Passive XP Sources

| Source | XP/hour | Conditions |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Daily Login | 100 | Once per day |
| Streak Bonus | 50-200 | Based on streak length |
| Season Passive | 10 | Per hour of play (capped) |

### XP Balance Philosophy

**Natural Play Expectation:**
- Casual player (1-2 hours/day): 20-30 levels per season
- Regular player (2-3 hours/day): 35-45 levels per season
- Dedicated player (3+ hours/day): 50 levels (full completion)

**Grinding Prevention:**
- No repeatable XP farm (caps on many sources)
- Quality over quantity in missions
- Events provide catch-up, not required grinding
- Casual players reach 70-80% of rewards naturally

## 5. Premium Track Philosophy

### Premium Value Proposition

**What Premium Track IS:**
- One-time purchase per season
- Cosmetic-only enhanced rewards
- Convenience bonuses (faster progress tracking, exclusive track view)
- Support for ongoing development

**What Premium Track is NOT:**
- Pay-to-win advantages
- Exclusive gameplay features
- Required for competitive play
- A requirement for enjoyment

### Premium Benefits

| Benefit | Free Track | Premium Track |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| Base XP | ✓ | ✓ (same rate) |
| Reward Levels | ✓ (Free tier only) | ✓ (Both tiers) |
| Season Cosmetics | ✗ | ✓ |
| Exclusive Frames | ✗ | ✓ |
| Progress Boosts | Limited | Enhanced tracking |
| Season Events | ✓ | ✓ (same access) |
| Competitive Play | ✓ | ✓ (no advantage) |

### Pricing Philosophy

**Season Pass Cost: $4.99 - $9.99 (varies by region)**

**Cost Justification:**
- Supports continued development
- Provides fair value for cosmetic content
- Affordable for most players
- Not required for core experience

**Value Comparison:**
- Individual cosmetics would cost more
- Battle Pass provides better overall value
- One purchase = entire season of content
- Legacy items retain value across seasons

## 10. AdsGram Integration

### Season Mode and AdsGram

AdsGram remains the primary revenue system. Season content respects this:

**Optional Rewards (Never Required):**
- Bonus Battle Pass XP (1.5x or 2x for limited time)
- Additional daily progress (extra mission refresh)
- Temporary progression boost (accelerated XP gain)
- Skip wait timers (if any implemented)

**Never Included in Season:**
- No required watching for season access
- No exclusive season content behind ads
- No competitive advantage for watching
- No pay-to-win elements

### Ad Examples for Season Mode

```
"Watch an ad to gain 100 Bonus XP toward Battle Pass"
"Watch to activate 2x XP Boost for the next hour"
"Watch to claim missed daily progress bonus"
```

### Ad Respect Guidelines

- Maximum 3 rewarded ads per season week
- Clear labeling of optional nature
- Easy dismiss option
- No artificial scarcity or urgency

## 11. Anti-FOMO Philosophy

### Time Respect Principles

**Jolt Time Should:**
- Respect that players have real lives
- Never require daily play to stay competitive
- Allow catch-up without excessive grinding
- Provide value for time invested

**Jolt Time Should NOT:**
- Force daily login to maintain progress
- Create anxiety about missing content
- Require hours of daily grinding
- Punish players for taking breaks

### Casual Player Support

**Realistic Expectations:**
- Casual player (1-2 hrs/day): 70-80% of rewards
- This includes all free track + some premium
- No reward is permanently lost (legacy system)
- Premium is cosmetic-only, not required

**Catch-Up Mechanisms:**
- Events provide bonus XP for behind players
- Weekend bonuses help catch up
- No daily login streak requirements
- Missed days don't compound penalties

### Balanced Progression

**Recommended vs. Required:**
- Recommended: Finish all dailies (10-20 min)
- Optional: Complete weekly missions (1-2 hrs)
- Optional: Participate in events (variable)
- Never required: Grinding for completion

**Season Completion Math:**
- 70 days of season
- 50 levels to complete
- ~35 levels natural for casual
- ~15 levels through events + light play
- 100% requires dedicated play, not grinding
